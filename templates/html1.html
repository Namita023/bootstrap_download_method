VISAKHAPATNAM

Beaches in Visakhapatnam (Vizag)

A scenic port city and industrial town, Visakhapatnam or Vizag boasts of a unsullied shoreline and lush greenery of the Eastern Ghats with temples and caves.

Borra Caves

Over 150 years old, the Borra Caves were discovered by British geologist William King in 1807 and comprise karstic limestone structures that are up to 80 metres deep.

Varaha Lakshmi Narasimha Temple

Also known as Simhachalam Temple, this 11th century temple sits on the Simhachalam Hill, about 300 metres above the sea level and is dedicated to the incarnation of Lord Vishnu..

Kailasagiri Park

At a height of 173 metres, the main attractions at Kailasagiri Park are 12-metre tall statues of Lord Shiva and goddess Parvathi, a floral clock and the Titanic viewpoint.
